Marry You

A typographic music video

This little animation was for my typography final
last semster. Our criterion was to make text move
in some way. When I was first introduced to this
assignment, I wanted to make something great or
exceptional. What I then realized was that what if
I make something fun and absurd instead? The
whole semester had been about following certain
rules and making it "perfect" while striving to get
an 'A' that I forgot to really have fun with my work
and do something for the heck of it. This was my
last project of that semester so I had nothing
but time (a whole weekend) to do so.
